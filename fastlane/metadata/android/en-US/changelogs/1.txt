Orpheus 0.1.0:

* First public FOSS release of Orpheus.
* Local playback, playlists, favorites, lyrics, listening stats, dynamic Material 3 theming, widgets, and backup/restore.
* Self-hosted Navidrome/Subsonic and Jellyfin library support, plus optional LRCLIB lyrics and Deezer artist artwork lookup.
* No Firebase, no Google Play Services, no proprietary integrations.
